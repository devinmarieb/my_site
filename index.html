As a frontend developer at the creative studio at WeTransfer, my work is centered around animations, interactive designs, and custom solutions. My team works with our sales representatives to bring client advertising designs to life, or build custom advertisements from scratch. I work directly with designers to create elements with Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects that will translate well to our advertising platform. I use libraries like GSAP and Anime.js to bring SVGs to life, and Lottie to animate After Effects files.

The creative studio also works on internal marketing projets and custom blog content that runs alongside our creative platform, WePresent. Over the past few years, I've worked on mini-sites like Craig Taylor: Anything but guns, an emotional story brought to life through art and code, as well as Ideas Report 2018, an insight project about creatives and their habits. These projects involve more traditional coding, with tools like React and Vue.

Ideas by WeTransfer is our company blog, which I maintain. This project uses a variety of technologies, including Contentful, a headless CMS, which gives us more control over the design and code.

Additionally, I was also the go-to developer for email templates before we had a dedicated email team. Working with SendGrid, Mailchimp and Intercom, I created custom email templates for our monthly newsletter, as well as product updates.
